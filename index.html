Mapgenbot: A Slackbot Integration

At Yik Yak, polls were a key form of user engagement - and a key source of data about the largely millennial user base. Making use of polls and geographic data, maps of opinions about the EU Referendum and sentiment right before the California primary are a couple of the many maps that told current events from Yik Yak users' perspectives. To streamline the map making process, I built a pipeline that took care of all the steps from data querying to visualization. I integrated the pipeline into a slackbot, Mapgenbot, that responded to simple direct messages that fed it a poll and some options for personalization (e.g. legend, colors).
